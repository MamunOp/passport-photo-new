NEET 2026 Photo Guidelines: Everything You Need to Know

By Mubasir • January 28, 2026

Writing a great intro here helps Google understand your page. Start by explaining the problem: "Uploading the wrong photo is the #1 reason for NEET form rejection."

1. Postcard Size Photo Requirements

The NTA requires a 4"x6" postcard-sized photograph. It must be in color and taken against a white background.

2. The Name and Date Stamp

This is the most important part. At the bottom of the photo, you must clearly print your name and the date of taking the photograph.

3. Face Coverage Rule

Ensure that your face covers at least 80% of the photograph. Your ears should be clearly visible, and you should not be wearing any mask, glasses, or cap.
