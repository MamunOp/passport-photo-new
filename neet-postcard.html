FAQs

1. Is white background mandatory for the postcard photo?

Yes, NTA rejects photos with colored or busy backgrounds. Ensure you upload a photo with a plain white background for the NEET application.

2. What is the difference between Passport and Postcard photo?

Passport photo is small (3.5x4.5cm), while Postcard photo is larger (4x6 inches). You need both for the NEET registration process.
