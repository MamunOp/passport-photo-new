NEET 2026 Official Guidelines

  • Dimensions: Exactly 4 inches x 6 inches (Postcard Size).
  • Stamp: Name of Candidate and Date of taking photo is mandatory.
  • Background: Must be white. No colored backgrounds allowed.
  • Format: JPG or JPEG only. (PNG not allowed on NTA portal).
  • Face Rules: 80% face coverage, visible ears, no caps/spectacles.
  • File Size: Between 10KB to 200KB.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the size of a NEET postcard photo in pixels?

For 4x6 inches at 300 DPI, the pixel size is 1200 x 1800 px. Our tool automatically sets this for you.

2. Is a name and date stamp mandatory for NEET?

Yes. As per NTA guidelines, both Postcard and Passport photos must have the candidate's name and date of photo taken at the bottom.

3. Can I use a mobile phone selfie?

No. Selfies are strictly rejected. Use a clear photo taken by someone else or a professional, with a white background.

4. Why does my photo keep getting rejected?

Common reasons: Wrong dimensions (not 4x6), missing name/date, colored background, or file size above 200KB.
