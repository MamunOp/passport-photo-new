Candidate Help & FAQs

Is a name and date mandatory for the NEET postcard?

Yes, as per NTA, the photo should have the name of the candidate and the date on which the photograph was taken printed at the bottom.

What if my photo background is not white?

You must use a white background. Photos with colored backgrounds are the #1 reason for NEET application rejection.

Can I use this tool on my smartphone?

Absolutely. Our tool is optimized for mobile cropping and supports iPhone HEIC formats automatically.
