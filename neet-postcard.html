Postcard Guidelines

Mandatory NTA technical requirements

Technical Specs

  • Dimensions: Exactly 4.0 inches (width) x 6.0 inches (height).
  • File Size: Minimum 10 KB to Maximum 200 KB.
  • Format: Strictly JPG or JPEG only.

Appearance Rules

  • Background: Plain white background is mandatory.
  • Stamp: Name of Candidate & Date must be printed.
  • Face: 80% face coverage, no mask/specs allowed.

How to use NEET Postcard Maker?

Step 1: Upload your existing passport-size photo by clicking the 'Upload' box.

Step 4: Adjust the quality slider if you need to reduce the file size below 200KB.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is the name and date stamp mandatory for NEET 2026?

Yes. As per the Information Bulletin, the photograph should have the name of the candidate and the date on which the photograph was taken printed on it.

2. Can I use a polaroid or selfie for the postcard photo?

No. Selfies or polaroids are strictly rejected. The photo should be clear, professionally taken, or well-lit with a plain white background.

3. What if I forget to add the name and date?

Your application may be rejected or kept on hold. It is highly recommended to use our tool to add the name and date stamp correctly.

4. Can I upload a 4x6 photo on my mobile?

Yes, our tool is fully mobile-friendly. You can crop, resize, and download the official 4x6 JPG directly to your phone.

5. Why does my photo look stretched after upload?

Our cropper tool locks the aspect ratio to 4:6. This ensures your photo is never stretched and meets official NTA dimensions perfectly.
