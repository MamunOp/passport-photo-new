NTA Photo Guidelines 2026

Technical

  • • Dimension: 4"x6" (Portrait).
  • • Resolution: 300 DPI (1200x1800 px).
  • • File Limit: 10KB to 200KB.

Visibility

  • • 80% face coverage is required.
  • • Ears must be visible.
  • • Background must be white.

FAQs

Is white background mandatory for NEET?

Yes, NTA strictly mandates a white background for both passport and postcard photographs.
