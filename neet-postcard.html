Official Photo Guidelines for NEET 2026: Complete Guide.

M

By Mubasir

Jan 28, 2026 • 6 min read

Applying for national exams like NEET, JEE, or UPSC can be stressful. One of the biggest hurdles candidates face is the strict photograph requirements. In 2026, the rules have become even more specific.

The Name and Date Stamp Rule

Most candidates forget that for certain exams, a simple selfie or a studio portrait isn't enough. You need to have your Full Name and the Date of the Photo printed clearly at the bottom.

Pro Tip

"Don't manually write on your photo. It looks unprofessional and might get rejected."

Key Technical Specifications

  • Background: Plain White (No colors allowed).
  • Face Coverage: At least 80% of the area.
  • File Format: Strictly JPG or JPEG.
  • File Size: Between 10KB to 200KB.

If your file is larger than 200KB, the application portal will show an "Upload Error." This is why using an exam photo resizer is essential. It compresses the file while keeping your face clear.

Final Checklist Before Upload

Before you hit that submit button, check if both your ears are visible and you aren't wearing any spectacles (unless you wear them regularly). Also, ensure the lighting is even—no shadows on the face!
