NEET Postcard Guidelines

Size & Format

  • • Dimension: Exactly 4 inches x 6 inches (Postcard).
  • • Format: Strictly JPG or JPEG format.
  • • File Size: Must be between 10KB to 200KB.

Appearance Rules

  • • Background: Plain white background only.
  • • Face: 80% face coverage, ears visible.
  • • Stamp: Name and Date mandatory at bottom.

FAQs

What is the postcard size for NEET in pixels?

For a high-quality 300 DPI print, the size is 1200 x 1800 pixels. Our tool automatically sets this for you.

Is the name and date stamp mandatory for 2026?

Yes. NTA requires the candidate's name and the date of taking the photograph to be clearly printed at the bottom of the photo.

Can I upload a PNG photo?

You can upload any image, but our tool will automatically convert it to the NTA-required JPG format upon download.

Will my photo be rejected if the background isn't white?

Yes. NTA is very strict about the white background. If your original photo has a colored background, please use a background remover before uploading here.
