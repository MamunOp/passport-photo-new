General Guidelines for
Exam Photos 2026

A small mistake in your exam photo can lead to form rejection. Follow these common standards used by most Indian exam authorities.

01

Plain White Background

90% of national exams (NTA, UPSC, IBPS) now require a plain white background. Colored or busy backgrounds are a major cause of rejection.

02

Size, Format & KB

Exam forms accept photos strictly in JPG/JPEG format. Always resize your image using an exam photo resizer to avoid upload errors.

An exam photo resizer is an online tool that helps you resize your photo to the correct passport size and file size (KB) required for exams like NEET, SSC, UPSC, JEE, and GATE.

Yes. This exam photo resizer online is completely free. You can upload, resize, and download your exam photo without signup or payment.

Yes. It helps you create a passport size photo for exams with the correct dimensions, background, and format accepted by exam portals.
