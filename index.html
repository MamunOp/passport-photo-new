Official 2026 Photo Guidelines

01

Background Protocol

Use a plain white background only. Avoid shadows on the wall or on the face. NTA and UPSC now strictly reject colored backgrounds.

02

Face Coverage

Ensure 80% face coverage. Both ears must be visible, and eyes must be looking directly at the camera with a neutral expression.
