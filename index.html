Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about exam photo registration.

Why did my photo get rejected by the exam portal?
Common reasons include blurry faces, non-white backgrounds, incorrect file sizes (too small/large), and wearing spectacles or caps. Use our tool to ensure the aspect ratio is perfect every time.
Is it safe to upload my photo here?
Yes! Unlike other websites, we do not upload your photo to a server. All editing happens inside your own browser (Chrome/Safari). Your privacy is our priority.
How do I add my name and date to my NEET photo?
Select the "NEET" tool from our homepage, upload your photo, and enter your details in the text boxes. Our tool will automatically overlay them in the official format before you download.
