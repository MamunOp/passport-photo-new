General Guidelines for Exam Photos 2026

Preparing for a competitive exam like NEET, SSC, or UPSC is hard enough. A small mistake in your exam photo can lead to form rejection. Follow these common exam photo guidelines 2026 used by most Indian exam authorities.

01.

Plain White Background

90% of national exams (NTA, UPSC, IBPS) now require a plain white background. Colored or busy backgrounds are a major cause of rejection.

02.

Correct Photo Size, Format & KB

Exam forms accept photos only in JPG/JPEG format with a strict file size (KB) limit. Always resize your image using an exam photo resizer to avoid upload errors.
