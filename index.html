Official Photo Guidelines 2026

When applying for national exams like NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) or SSC CGL, your photograph is the primary way the authority identifies you. Using an incorrect size can lead to immediate disqualification.

NEET Postcard Photo Requirements:

  • The photograph must be 4"x6" (Postcard Size).
  • It must be in color with a white background.
  • The candidate's name and date of taking the photo must be printed at the bottom.
  • 80% face coverage with visible ears.

SSC & Banking Photo Standards:

For SSC, the photo should not be more than three months old from the date of publication of the Notice of Examination. The photograph should be without cap, spectacles, and both ears should be visible.

FAQs

Why use 4x6 for NEET?

NTA requires a larger postcard-sized photo (4"x6") to be pasted on the proforma during the examination for physical verification of the candidate.
