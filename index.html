Official Exam Guidelines 2026

Preparing for a competitive exam like NEET, SSC, or UPSC is hard enough. Don't let a simple photograph lead to your application being rejected. Every government body has its own unique set of rules.

01.

The White Background Rule

90% of national exams (NTA, UPSC, IBPS) now require a plain white background. Colored or busy backgrounds are a major cause of rejection.

02.

Face Coverage Standards

Your face must cover at least 75-80% of the photo area. Ears must be clearly visible, and the head must be centered.
