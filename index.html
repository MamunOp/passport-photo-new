Guidelines for
Exam Photos 2026

A small mistake in your photo can lead to form rejection. Follow these three golden standards.

01

White Background

90% of national exams require a plain white background. Avoid shadows or colored walls.

02

Size & KB Limit

Photos must be JPG/JPEG and usually between 20KB to 50KB.

03

Name & Date Stamp

Exams like NEET often require your name and capture date printed at the bottom.
