Banking Exam FAQ

1. Can I wear glasses in the IBPS/SBI photo?

If you wear glasses, ensure there are no reflections and your eyes are clearly visible. However, IBPS/SBI often recommends taking photos without glasses to avoid rejection risk.

2. Why does my photo look blurry?

This happens if the source image is very low quality. Upload a high-resolution photo; our tool will downscale it to exactly 200x230 pixels while maintaining clarity.

3. What if my file size is 15KB?

IBPS/SBI systems will not accept anything below 20KB. Move the quality slider to the right (higher quality) until the estimate is above 20KB.
