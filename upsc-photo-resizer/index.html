UPSC OTR FAQ

1. Is it okay if I don't print my name on the UPSC photo?

No. UPSC guidelines 2024-25 clearly state that the application will be rejected if the name and date of the photo are not mentioned on the image.

2. Can I use a photo from last year for OTR?

No. UPSC requires the photo to be no more than 10 days old. You must upload a fresh photo for every new notification or OTR update.
