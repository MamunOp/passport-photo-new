Privacy Policy

At ExamPhoto Pro, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by ExamPhoto Pro and how we use it.

1. Local Image Processing

ExamPhoto Pro processes all images locally in your browser. This is a security feature. When you upload a photo, it never travels to our servers. All resizing happens on your device CPU. Your data never leaves your device.

2. Log Files

We follow standard procedures of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes IP addresses, browser type, ISP, and date/time stamps. These are not linked to any personal identity.

Third-party ad servers use cookies or JavaScript for their advertisements. ExamPhoto Pro has no access to or control over these cookies used by third-party advertisers.
