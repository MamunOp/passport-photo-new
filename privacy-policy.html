Privacy Policy

At ExamPhoto Pro, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by ExamPhoto Pro and how we use it.

1. Local Image Processing

Unlike traditional online tools, ExamPhoto Pro processes all images locally in your browser. This means your photos are never uploaded to our servers, stored in any database, or viewed by our team. Your data stays on your device.

2. Log Files

ExamPhoto Pro follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes IP addresses, browser type, ISP, and date/time stamps.

3. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our website and other sites on the internet.

4. Advertising Partners

Third-party ad servers use technologies like cookies or JavaScript for their respective advertisements. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs to measure the effectiveness of their campaigns.

5. Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor and guide their online activity.
