Recency Policy

02

No Accessories

Photographs with caps, spectacles, or frontal face not clearly visible will be rejected.

SSC Photo FAQ

1. What happens if my photo is less than 20KB?

The SSC server will reject the upload. Use our quality slider to ensure the estimated size is between 20KB and 50KB.

2. Do I need to print the date on the SSC photo?

As per the latest SSC CGL/CHSL notifications, a date stamp is NOT mandatory, but the photo must be recent. If you still want it, our tool provides an optional stamp.
