Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is the date stamp required on the SSC photo?

No. As per the latest official SSC recruitment notices, the date and name stamp on the photograph is no longer mandatory. The photo must simply be recent.

2. Can I upload a photo with spectacles?

No. SSC strictly rejects photos with spectacles (glasses), even if you wear them regularly. Ensure your eyes and ears are clearly visible.

3. How do I get exactly 20KB-50KB?

Use the quality slider in our tool. It provides a live size estimate. Aim for around 35KB to be safe.
